About

My Story

Hi, I’m Simmi, a Munich-based UX/UI designer focused on creating accessible, user-centered digital experiences. I recently completed Career Foundry’s UI Design Program where I acquired skills in visual design, prototyping, usability testing and design systems. I also learned and worked on tools like Figma, FigJam, Miro and Lyssna. I delivered projects across various domains like e-commerce, lifestyle, wellness and productivity.

I am now starting a specialization in Frontend Development for Designers. I choose this specialization because understanding the foundations of programming brings valuable insights into how to work with design considerations alongside technical requirements. It helps in bridging design and development to build even stronger design systems.

With background in education, I bring problem-solving, presentation and strong collaboration skills that help me communicate clearly with both users and across teams.

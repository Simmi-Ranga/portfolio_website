Tools

During the UX/UI design process for the Bliss App, various tools were used to aid in collaboration, design creation, progress tracking, and user behavior analysis. Figma was utilized for creating wireframes and high-fidelity prototypes, InVision for interactive prototypes, Miro for brainstorming sessions and ideation process, and Lysnna for user behavior analysis.

Overall, these tools played a crucial role in the efficient design process of the Bliss App, enabling the team to collaborate effectively, design a user-friendly interface, track progress, and analyze user behavior, while optimizing the app's features and functionality.

Target Audience

Target Audience: Adults aged between 25-45 years old mostly students and working professionals who are motivated to live a healthy lifestyle by following a healthy routine with healthy habits but are over whelmed by apps that are either not easy to navigate and also must requires a paid subscription

About the App

Bliss is a mental wellness app dedicated to helping individuals combat stress and sleep - related issues through the power of meditation and mindfulness. Bliss is designed to provide a gentle space for users to pause, breathe and reconnect with themselves. It helps users check-in with their emotions, sleep patterns and practice mindful breathing & doing meditation. With a collection relaxing Sleep music, soothing ambience music and valuable guided sessions, Bliss will help the users enrich their mental well-being and promotes healthy lifestyle.

Problem

We deal with problems like stress, anxiety and burnout on a daily basis and these issues are only growing worse with time. In today's fast paced world, finding time for mental well-being feels nearly impossible. People struggle with problems like insomnia, lack of focus, negative thoughts and constant fatigue..

Solution

The goal is to create a wellness app which is free and designed to support mental well-being. It helps users track their moods, sleep patterns, reflect on emotions and practice self-care through meditation, sleep music, soundscapes and workout videos based on the users own challenges. The purpose of the app is to promote healthy living by forming healthy habits of the users by guiding them on their meditation journey and create a positive, consistent routine..

Design Process

I adopted the design thinking framework for the design process. This involved empathizing with users, defining the problem, ideating, prototyping, and implementing. User research and feedback helped us understand their needs and constraints, and I generated design ideas through brainstorming and collaboration. Prototypes were tested with real users to gather feedback for iterative design improvements.
